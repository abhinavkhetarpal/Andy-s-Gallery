I'm Andy Kasper

Thank you for checking out my portfolio. It is a collection of paintings and photographs that each mean something to me. I believe that art is in the process, not necessarily in the product, so please feel free to use my art in whatever way you feel drawn. Each of the works featured in this collection were an experience and experiment of creation with the tools and media available to me at the time.

I invite you to explore these works, see if they evoke any ideas or feelings, and please share any comments or stories you may have using the contact. Originals can be purchased where available.
